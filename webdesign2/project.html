Hybrid Classification System for Sleep Apnea

I devised a model that helps doctors assess sleep problems such as sleep apnea, irregular limb movements, narcolepsy, restless legs syndrome, insomnia, and night-time behaviours. Such conditions will also not be detected during a regular visit to the clinic. This sleeping disorder may lead to heart failure, diabetes, increased blood pressure and may even cause death. We have developed a system to classify normal patient and a sleep apnea patient by utilising EEG and ECG signals of 18 patients.

Time-Series analysis for sales forecasting using hybrid SARIMA
